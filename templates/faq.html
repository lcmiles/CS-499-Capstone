Frequently Asked Questions

Need help navigating Blazing Adoptions? These sections might help:

  • Can I browse pets without creating an account?
    Yes, you can view pet listings without registering.
  • Can I save pets as a guest?
    No, you will need to register for this feature.
  • Will the pets I see be up-to-date?
    Yes, listings are updated in real time by shelter management.
  • How do I save a pet?
    Click on the save/heart button on a pet listing. You can view saved pets in your profile.
  • How do I update my profile info?
    Click your profile picture in the top-right and select “Edit Profile.”
  • How do I follow another user?
    Visit their profile and click the "Follow" button.
  • How do I apply to adopt a pet?
    Navigate to the pet's page and click the "Adopt" button. Fill out the adoption application form and submit it.
  • How do I check the status of my adoption application?
    Go to the "Adoption Applications" section in your profile to view the status of your applications.
  • What happens after my application is approved?
    You will be contacted by the shelter or individiual to schedule a pet pickup or further steps.
  • How do I request a new shelter?
    Go to the "Shelter Requests" page and fill out the form with the shelter's details. Submit the request for admin approval.
  • How do I add staff to my shelter?
    Go to the "Manage Shelter" page and use the "Request to Add Staff" form to invite new staff members. Those sent invitations must accept to be added to staff.
  • How do I approve or deny adoption requests?
    Navigate to the "Adoption Requests" section in the "Manage Shelter" page. Use the "Approve" or "Deny" buttons for each request.
  • How do I add a pet to my shelter?
    Go to the "Manage Shelter" page and click "Add Pet." A list of pets avaible to be added will be displayed. Only pets posted by members of shelter staff can be added.
